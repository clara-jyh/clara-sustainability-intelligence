# Clara Sustainability Intelligence (CSI)

> **From Information to Insight.**

Clara Sustainability Intelligence (CSI) is a knowledge platform dedicated to sustainability, Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG), University Social Responsibility (USR), and the circular economy.

Here, **intelligence** refers to verified information that has been analyzed, connected, and transformed into actionable insight.

Rather than simply collecting news, CSI organizes trusted public information into structured knowledge that supports better decisions for universities and sustainable development.

## Focus

- 🌏 Sustainability
- 🎓 University Social Responsibility (USR)
- ♻️ Circular Economy
- 📊 Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG)
- 🌱 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)
- 🌍 Climate Action & Adaptation

## Why CSI?

Information is everywhere.

Insight is rare.

CSI bridges the gap by turning reliable information into meaningful knowledge that is easier to understand, connect, and apply.

## Mission

To provide evidence-based sustainability intelligence that helps universities, educators, researchers, and sustainability practitioners stay informed, identify emerging trends, and transform information into action.

## Status

🚧 This project is under active development.

New reports, knowledge resources, and platform features will continue to be released over time.
